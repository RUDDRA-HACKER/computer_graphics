## Computer Graphics
**The term 'Computer Graphics' was coined by Verne Hudson in 1960. It refers to the field of visual computing, where one utilizes computers to create, manipulate, and represent visual images and animations. Computer graphics encompasses a wide range of techniques and applications, including 2D and 3D modeling, rendering, animation, and visualization. It plays a crucial role in various industries such as entertainment, design, education, and scientific research. The development of computer graphics has been driven by advancements in hardware and software technologies, enabling more realistic and complex visual representations.**
